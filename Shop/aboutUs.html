About Us

CLoudPal was founded in 2016 by entrepeneur, irish enthusiast and lasagne lover, Eimear Broderick. The company was created with the end goal of making cloud services easy to purchase and understand for the everyday consumer. Since then the business has grown from strength to strength and is now ranked in the top 5 CLoud Service Providers in Europe, employing 35 innovative humans who strive to make cloud computing as enjoyable and accesible as possible.
