Sports

Favourite Sport: Gaelic Football
I started playing football with my local club when I was 7 years old. Most of the team know each other since these early days so there is great comradery. There has always been a big interest in GAA on both sides of my family so it was a given that my siblings and I would get involved in some way, shape or form. I love that it keeps me active and forces me to get out into the fresh air, even on the coldest January evenings

Training: Tuesdays and Fridays


Other Interests: Ice Skating
I started working in an ice rink when I was 17 an practiced my skating after every shift. Since then I have improved year by year and have just finished my third season working there. We play ice hockey on occasion and this is particularly exciting as we have some of my co-workers are very talented at the sport.
